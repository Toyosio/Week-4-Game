Crystal Collector



You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a cystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number.

You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The vale of the crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, values of each crystal will change!
